WHO ARE WE?
Formed in February 2011, Enactus DTU is a dedicated team of students with a head for business and a heart for the world. The team truly believes in empowerment of the weaker sections of the society through business acumen and diligence. It is this belief which has allowed Enactus DTU to run over 10 flourishing projects across the NCR. Enactus DTU has constantly endeavored to be the mascot of change and revolution, and bring innovative entrepreneurial solutions to the table in matters concerning the various evils afflicting the Indian society. This approach has brought Enactus multiple laurels of success. Enactus DTU represented India in the World Cup in 2013 and recently placed 3rd in India, in the Enactus National Competition 2017. We secured the fifth position in the Enactus National Competition 2018 out of 24 competing teams.

PAST PROJECTS

PROJECT UNNAYAN

Unnayan is a water based project where Enactus DTU has developed a biosand filter in collaboration with Sehgal Foundation and plans on selling these filters and setting them up in urban slums where drinking water is expensive or not available. The aim of Unnayan is to provide safe drinking water to the under privileged and reduce diseases due to consumption of unhealthy water.

PROJECT AVATARAN

Project Avataran was an Enactus DTU initiative that taught the acid attack victims to make liquid dishwashing soaps. The soap was made by recycling and reusing cooking oil that would have otherwise been wasted or recooked, leading to potentially harmful environmental and physiological effects.

PROJECT CHAAP

Project Chhaap was started as a venture to support and empower the women of this country, to provide them with a source of livelihood, by printing, for printing custom-designed sweatshirts and t-shirts. Project Chhaap, have proven that innovation, combined with perseverance, always wins.These women have not only earned a stable source of income but also, a lot of respect and honour in their society.
